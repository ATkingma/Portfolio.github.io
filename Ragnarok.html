About ​Ragnarok

Ragnarok was an school project at my school. My main role was to get the map working with an grass shader and also an map that usses an seed that wil be send to al the players. The goal in Ragnarok is to survive and get as strong as posible we added 5 bosses and an end boss first you  will need to activate 5 totems where the mini bosses wil spawn out of and then the main boss wil spawn. you can progress in the game by killing enemies where you wil get xp and gold from gold you can then by opening chests you also get bonnus gold and an item if you activate an normal totem. 
you can also craft in the game and progress by gathering and upgrading your tools.

futher more we ussed photon for multieplayer and an procedual map generated by an noisemap for our map
we had 10 weeks for this project and the group 3 people big with 1 artist and 1 other programmer next to me. We wanted to make an multieplayer game where we could learn a lot from like how multieplayer could be the best optimisted. this was an fun project and i loved testing this.

Procedual map

the map works with an noisemap this wil be created and an part of it wil be ussed to create an falloffmap afther the falloffmap is generated triangels wil be created and verts wil be set after this the verts wil be asigned an collor from an gradient and this will be saved on an temperary material. after this proces the enviroment wil be spawned then if everythink is finish the mesh wil be coppied and an grass shader wil be aplied then the host will be baking the navmesh and the players wil be spawned when that is done.
