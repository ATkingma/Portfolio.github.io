Privacy Policy

Summary: ActivityTracker operates entirely on your device. All data is stored locally on your computer, and we never collect, transmit, or share your browsing data with any third parties.

What Data We Collect

ActivityTracker collects the following information:

  • Site patterns that you manually add to track
  • Time spent on websites matching your saved patterns
  • Dates and duration of visits to tracked websites

We don't collect any personal information or any data about specific page content you view. We only track the time spent on domains you've explicitly chosen to monitor.

How Your Data is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This means:

  • Your browsing data never leaves your computer
  • We don't use any remote servers to store your information
  • No one else can access your activity data unless they have physical access to your device
  • If you uninstall the extension, all tracked data is permanently deleted

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere, security risks are minimal. The extension only has access to the specific permissions granted in the installation process.

Your Control Over Your Data

You have complete control over your data:

  • You choose which websites to track
  • You can remove tracked sites at any time
  • You can clear all stored data by uninstalling the extension
  • You can see exactly what data is stored through the extension's interface

Third-Party Access

ActivityTracker doesn't share data with any third parties. There are no analytics, tracking pixels, or remote servers involved in its operation.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the version number of the extension.
